SRKade is a USA/UK based emulation server that brings people together to play classic games online.

This server was founded by Bev and BluETurbo back in 2019.

In 2023, A Fatal Bon Bon & ProtonicCobra were handed control of the server and have kept pushing it forward.

Note: While we specialize in fighting games (Tekken, Mortal Kombat, Street Fighter, etc.), we also enjoy exploring other genres now and then. These titles use delay-based netcode which was a lot more common before rollback became the standard. Online play reflects the latency & sync behavior typical of games from the 2000–2010 era.
