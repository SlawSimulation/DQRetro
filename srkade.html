SRKade is a USA/UK based emulation server that brings people together to play classic games online.

This server was founded by Bev and BluETurbo back in 2019.

In 2023, A Fatal Bon Bon, ProtonicCobra, and Squid were handed control of the server and have kept pushing it forward.

While we specialize in fighting games (Tekken, Mortal Kombat, Street Fighter, etc.), we also enjoy exploring other genres now and then.

Note: These titles use delay-based netcode, which was standard for most games released prior to widespread rollback adoption. Online performance reflects typical latency and synchronization methods used in the 2000–2010 era.
